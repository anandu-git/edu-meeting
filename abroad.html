Canada

Canada, the city of maple leaf, is a hot spot for international students. It is a great place which accepts multi cultures and a place to grow for international students and has a vibrant campuslife. Canada has many world renowned universities with a high graduate employability rate and good academic performance. It is the tenth largest economy in the world. Moreover, Canada is an English speaking country, so you can easily survive by speaking and communicating in English. Canada has grown in popularity as a study destination for overseas students, particularly in recent years. Canadian academic institutions also readily accept English language proficiency tests other than IELTS which you can submit during the admission process. To assist excellent international students in funding their education, Canadian universities and colleges provide a variety of scholarships. Aside from that, the Canadian government, as well as public and private institutions, offer several scholarships, grants, and bursaries. Canada is also well known for recognizing talented students. The eligible candidates get financial aids in the form of grants and scholarships. In addition to a high-quality educational system, the Canadian education focuses primarily on research, scientific publications, and international cooperation. Still worried thinking of the tiring long procedure for getting your study permit in Canada? Not anymore. Here is a Student Direct Scheme, which allows faster processing of your application which streamlines the process as a whole. This initiative by the Canadian Immigration Authorities, help the students apply for Visa under the SDS category. These applications get processed within 20 calendar days. The other category of applications is hence called General Category. So if you have a dream to study in Canada, Medcity Study Abroad will help you live your dreams.

United Kingdom

The sovereign state of United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland will take you through its vast rich history of both industrial revolution and modern parliamentary democracy. The country has become one of the most popular destinations when it comes to studying abroad with high quality education system, and job opportunities all over. Since the nation comprises the land of England, Wales, Scotland and North Ireland, it offers you with a perfect blend of multiple cultures. Its capital city London, is one of the largest financial centers in the world and has wide opportunities in arts, commerce, science and technology. The nation has so much to offer to their international students with its institutions and universities consistently ranked amongst the best in the world. UK has 4 out of the top 10 universities in the world and it has some of the oldest Universities in any English speaking country. The world class education system and enumerable choice of courses with international recognition has always flooded students to the nation. Many of the universities in the UK have an eased eligibility criteria from accepting students with backlogs to providing an IELTS waiver option for various courses. The requirements the students will have to fulfill are less as compared to other countries. The courses offered are of shorter duration than anywhere else in the world and thus provide value for both time and money spent in the country. The international students also have the opportunity to work 20 hours per week during term time and 40 hours per week during vacations which favor them in meeting their living cost. Students here can enjoy flexibility not only in terms of part-time work, but also enable them to blend their academic courses with vocational interests like extracurricular activities.The UK has some interesting courses that are also high on demand. But choosing the right one can mean taking into account various aspects, or more precisely, the career prospects of the course rather than your liking of it. Studying in the UK has been the dream of many. Top universities and career prospects make it the perfect destination for international students. However, Student visa applications can be a troublesome task for the ones who have no experience on that front. Universities of the UK also provide financial aid to their international students in the form of scholarships.Commonwealth Scholarships, the Chevening Scholarships, Rhodes Scholarship are some of the top scholarships available for international students. The educational system in the UK also offers various health benefits to students in terms of free or subsidized medical treatment, all making it the best destination for students who wish to study in UK.
